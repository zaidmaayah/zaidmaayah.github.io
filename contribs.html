My Contributions to Berkeley, and Beyond

Data 8 Staff

Timeframe: January 2021-Present

After enjoying and excelling in Data 8 in Fall 2020, I sought to get involved with the course staff. In Spring 2021 I served as an Academic Intern, whereby I assisted students during their lab sections and gained valuable advice from senior student instructors. Following that experience, I was appointed to be a Tutor in Fall 2021. This entails leading a small-group tutoring section, grading assignments, and supervising Office Hours. Once the semester starts I will be uploading my teaching resources here.
